We believe that there is a lack of information behind acing standardized testing. There is a certain mysticism regarding students who score in the top percentiles on the SATs and ACTs. Our goal as tutors is to erase that mysticism and provide our experience, as people who have scored in the top 99% of these tests, to our students. Our philosophy on standardized testing comes in the form of three pillars.

The first pillar is allowing adequate time for preparation:

There is no substitute for putting in the work and time; however, this by itself does not guarantee high scores. Mentorprep seeks to utilize the amount of time every student has and optimize it to their specific testing needs.

The second pillar is having proper understanding of the test

These standardized tests are written every year, and there are a limited number of questions that can be asked. Thus, we believe in understanding the questions and the basics of what is going to be a sked which can be accomplished through our directed preparation. As people who have done thousands and thousands of practice questions, we believe that we are capable of imparting these tips and tricks to our students.

The last pillar we have is our support system and understanding

We hope to be tutors that not only teach strategies for standardized testing, but also how to handle the mentally and physically draining aspects that come with the many hours of studying and taking these rigorous tests. As people who have recently experienced the grueling process, we can encourage and motivate our students and hope to act not only as a mentor but also as a part of their support system.
